### About

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics and an Honours degree in Computer Science from the University of the Witwatersrand.

Today, I work as a **DevOps and Platform Engineer**, specialising in the **BACK stack** — Backstage, ArgoCD, Crossplane, and Kyverno. My focus is on building internal developer platforms and scalable cloud-native infrastructure that gets out of developers' way.

I started my career as an API developer, spending over five years in the **.NET/C# ecosystem** before pivoting into the Linux and cloud-native world.

This blog is where I document what I'm learning, building, and occasionally breaking.
